Appendix A

Due to the high number of missing working impressions, we conducted a sensitivity analysis including data that was matched without working impression as a variable. Figure 5 shows the change in proportion of appropriate non-conveyance in this group and can be compared with Figure 4. The results of the segmented regression can be seen in Table 5.
